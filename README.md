# Sales-Revenue-Dashboard

## 1.Project Overview
   This project is an interactive Sales & Revenue Dashboard developed using Power BI and Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across different regions, product categories, and customer segments. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key business metrics through dynamic visualizations, helping stakeholders monitor revenue trends, profitability, and regional performance for informed decision-making.

## 2. Tech Stack
 * Power BI Desktop
 * Microsoft Excel
 * Power Query
 * DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

## 3.Features 

*  Business Problem: Organizations often generate large volumes of sales data from multiple regions and product categories, making it difficult to track business      performance using raw datasets. A centralized dashboard is required to monitor sales, profit, customer performance, and regional trends, enabling businesses   to identify growth opportunities and improve decision-making.

*  Goal of the Dashboard
   - Monitor overall sales, profit, quantity sold, and profit margin.
   - Compare sales performance across different regions.
   - Analyze monthly sales and profit trends.
   - Identify top-performing product categories, products, and customers.
   - Visualize state-wise sales distribution to support regional business analysis.

* Walkthrough of Key Visuals
   - KPI Cards: Display Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Profit Margin for a quick performance overview.
   - Regional Filter: Allows users to analyze sales across Central, East, South, and West regions.
   - Monthly Sales & Profit Charts: Show year-wise sales and profit trends for performance comparison.
   - State-wise Sales Map: Visualizes geographic sales distribution across different states.
   - Category & Sub-Category Analysis: Highlights sales contribution by product categories and sub-categories.
   - Top Customers & Products: Identifies the highest-performing customers and best-selling products.

* Business Impact & Insights
Identifies high-performing regions and product categories.
Tracks monthly sales and profit trends to support forecasting.
Highlights top customers and products contributing to business growth.
Enables geographic analysis of sales performance for regional planning.
Supports data-driven decisions to improve profitability and optimize sales strategies.
